Nerdma (Pty) Ltd is a leading Level 1 BBB-EE, IT Solutions company. We offer a wide range of IT solutions across the Southern Africa. We have a proud legacy of excellence and reliability that our clients, suppliers, staff and shareholders are proud of.

Our clients include multinational companies, listed corporates, small to medium entities, non-profit organisations and government departments. Nerdma has a highly skilled and dynamic team that, individually and collectively, has experience across a range of industries - Financial Services, Health, Agriculture, Manufacturing and Engineering. This experience makes us uniquely positioned to offer solutions that guarantee greater operational efficiency, productivity gains and cost savings for our clients.
