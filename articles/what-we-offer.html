Resourcing

We provide clients with skilled personnel on a permanent or contract basis. Through our in-house training we provide competent Project Managers, Software Developers, Cybersecurity Experts, Data Engineers, Digital Transformation Experts, Network Specialists, ERP Consultants, Testers, Business and Systems Analysts. Our resources have extensive experience across a wide range of industries sectors in both, private and public sectors.

Custom Software Development

We develop custom software applications for enterprise, web, desktop and mobile platforms. We work across open-source and proprietary technologies. Nerdma follows an Agile software development approach which allows our teams to develop and test the software real time in line with client specifications throughout the process.

Business Continuity Planning

We create systems of prevention and recovery to deal with threats that are specific to your organisation. Furthermore, we ensure that you continue with your business operations in the event of disaster through cloud recovery solutions and/or traditional disaster recovery sites.

Infrastructure & Enterprise Architecture Consultancy

Enterprise Architecture aligns your organization's IT infrastructure with your overall business goals. Nerdma will assist in the analysis, designing, planning and implementation at an enterprise level. This will allow the technical systems and infrastructure to support business requirements.

Cybersecurity

We offer a wide range of cybersecurity solutions that protect your entity's computer systems. This includes DevSecOps that embed security into code real-time. Our solutions include training and support of client staff which has proved to be helpful in securing IT and Network systems. We have formal partnerships with leading security organisations including Mimecast who provide best in class email security amongst other features.
